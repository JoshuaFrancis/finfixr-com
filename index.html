Platform features

Three modules that close the gap between knowing and fixing.

01

Cash flow intelligence

Automated bank feed aggregation categorises every transaction and builds a rolling 90-day cash flow picture. Predictive modelling flags upcoming shortfalls before they become overdrafts. Recurring expense tracking surfaces subscription creep and unused services that are quietly eroding monthly cash position without triggering any obvious alert.

02

Debt paydown engine

Users input all credit card, line of credit, student loan, and mortgage balances. The engine runs avalanche and snowball payoff simulations, calculates total interest saved under each strategy, and generates a month-by-month paydown schedule that adapts as balances and interest rates change over time.

03

Net worth tracking

Live net worth calculation connecting assets and liabilities from every connected account: chequing, savings, TFSA, RRSP, brokerage, mortgage, and vehicle loan. Historical net worth chart shows progress over time and benchmarks the user's trajectory against savings targets and retirement readiness milestones.
